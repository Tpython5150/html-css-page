Jui Jitsu

In Seattle Washnigton I began training jui-jitsu. The schools range from James Fosters jui-jitsu, to a small school by the University of Washington called Mat Chess MMA. At first it was slow going. I also worked at ups, bounced at bars and also worked at City Hall of Seattle as security. Weight training was also essential, everyday building my strength. Eventually I moved to Albuqeruque, New Mexico. I began training at Gracie Barra, to continue my skills.

MMA

Once I moved to Albuquerque, training with Gracie Barra I became an MMA Fighter. Started MMA taining at Jackson Wink Academy. At first the amatuer school, and then at the Pro Academy. Then after some time I went to Los Angeles to study boxing at Wild Card. Freddie Roach was the trainer of Mannny Pacquio. My trainer was Justin Juko, who fought Mayweather in 2015. This was the second time my nose was bloodied.

Santa Fe Ski Area

When I was 25yrs old, I was a snowboard enthusiast. Santa Fe Ski Area was one of the best mountains at which I boarded. Wolf Creek in Pagossa Springs, Colorado was also a great mountain to visit. Although trips has to end when the weekend was over, good times were had. The best experience was a night we missed the last tram car to the bottom, my brother and friends snowboarded down the mountain in fresh powder and the moonlight. Perfect! My freind gave us a ride around the mountain to where we were parked. An ironman race is in the near future!
